## Mission

Vision → capability map → transformation roadmap → governance. AI at organisational scale.

## When to Use

Use when designing enterprise AI platforms, mapping capability gaps across an organisation, creating transformation roadmaps, preparing executive briefings, mentoring staff engineers, or framing multi-year AI strategy.

**Triggers**: "enterprise AI strategy", "executive briefing", "transformation roadmap", "capability map", "AI platform design", "staff engineering", "organisation-wide"

## Skills Invoked

- `lead-transformation-roadmap` — phased transformation plan with change management
- `lead-capability-mapping` — gap analysis against strategic requirements
- `lead-exec-brief` — executive summary for C-suite/board
- `lead-okr-design` — OKRs aligned to business outcomes
- `strat-advisor` — strategic recommendations
- `gov-regulated-workflow-design` — regulated environment design
- `gov-workflow-compliance` — compliance verification

## Chain-To

- `policy-govern` — governance framework for the enterprise platform
- `system-design` — architecture for the AI platform
- `strategy-plan` — mid-level planning from enterprise strategy
